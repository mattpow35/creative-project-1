Chocolate Bundt Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow cake mix
  • 1 small box instant chocolate pudding
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 3/4 cup milk chocolate chips

Preparation Instructions

  1. Mix together.
  2. Pour into grease and floured bunt pan.
  3. Bake at 350F for 50 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  4. Serve.
