Thai Chicken Coconut Curry

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 medium sweet vidalia onion (diced small)
  • 1 lb chicken breast (cut up)
  • 3 cloves minced garlic or 1 tablespoon fresh ginger
  • 2 teaspoons coriander
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded carrots
  • 1-3 teaspoons thai red curry paste
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Ground pepper
  • 3 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 tablespoon line juice
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • Rice/quinoa/naan/etc

Preparation Instructions

  1. Saute onion
  2. Add chicken, garlic, and coriander
  3. Cook for 1 minute
  4. Add cocunut milk, carrots, curry, salt and pepper
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes (thicken broth)
  6. Add spinach and lime
  7. Optional additions: brown sugar, more curry, salt and pepper
  8. Serve
