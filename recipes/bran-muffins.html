Bran Muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 5 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup butter (or shortening)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 quart buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups All Bran
  • 2 cups 40% Bran Flakes

Preparation Instructions

  1. Combine the water and baking soda, set aside to cool.
  2. Cream the sugar and butter together.
  3. Add eggs one at a time. Add water mixture and buttermilk.
  4. In seperate large bowl, mix flour salt, All Bran, and bran flakes.
  5. Stir in butter mixture. Tightly cover.
  6. Referidgerate 12 hrs.
  7. Bake at 375' F for 20 minutes.
