Huevos Mexicanos

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 1/4 white onion
  • 1 serrano pepper
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 sprig cilantro
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt plus to taste

Preparation Instructions

  1. Chope the onion, tomato, serrano chile, garlic, and cilantro.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan to medium. Add the cooking oil and then add the onion.
  3. Cook for 1 minute. Stir the mixture each time you add an ingredient.
  4. Add the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add the serrano chile and cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add the tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Add the cilantro.
  8. Crack 4 large eggs into a bowl and season with a pinch of salt.
  9. Beat the eggs lightly with a fork and poor into the pan with the vegetables.
  10. Allow the eggs to set slightly which will take about two minutes.
  11. After they have set, stir the eggs gently until they are cooked through.
  12. Serve immediately with some refreid beans and warm tortillas.
